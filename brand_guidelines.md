# ABYSS Brand Guidelines (starter)

Core:
- Name: ABYSS
- Tagline: Absolute Control. Absolute Silence.
- Sub-brand: ABYSS by MIDAS

Colors:
- #050505 — Obsidian (primary background)
- #1B0036 — Deep violet
- #0F3D3E — Deep teal
- #C7B27C — Elite gold (accent)
- #AAAAAA — Silver (text accents)

Typography:
- Heading: elegant serif (e.g., Playfair Display / similar)
- UI: Inter / system sans for clarity

Logo:
- Primary: black circle with gold thin vertical crack + central dot, text: "ABYSS by MIDAS" below
- Variants: icon-only (circle), wordmark-only

Usage:
- Dark backgrounds preferred
- Generous negative space
- Minimal animations (subtle glow on hover)
